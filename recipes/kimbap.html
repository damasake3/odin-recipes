Kimbap

Description

Kimbap the dish is essentially rice wrapped in seaweed and filled with a variety of ingredients. The term kimbap translates directly to "seaweed and rice." Kimbap is a traditional dish that goes back several centuries and has been satisfying Korean foodies throughout the ages.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Danmuji
  • Fried Egg
  • Fish Cake
  • Marinated Beef
  • Rice seasoned with salt, vinegar, and sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Rice Cooker
  2. Chop Your Ingredients
  3. Cook Your Eggs and Meat
  4. Spread Your Rice Over a Rolling Mat
  5. Arrange Your Ingredients in Tidy Rows
