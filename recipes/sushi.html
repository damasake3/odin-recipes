Sushi

Description

Homemade salmon roll made with sushi rice, nori, and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi Paste
  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice, mix in vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate
  2. Assemble the sushi rolls
  3. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces
